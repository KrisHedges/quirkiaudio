Full-Featured Audio Synthesis Using Web Technology

Quirki QM3

Monophonic Analogue Modeling Synthesizer

  • Features a 3 Oscillator (VCO) design each with their own suboscilators and independent Envelope.
  • 2 low frequency oscillators (LFO) for modulation.
  • A low-pass and high-pass filter section with independent envelopes.
  • A mixer section for each oscillator and suboscillator.
  • An oscilliscope to help you visualize and carve out the perfect waveform.
  • Midi input for use with external keyboards and controllers.
